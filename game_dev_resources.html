Game Dev Resources

This website brings together a list of online resources that a game developer requires. Most of these are free. Irrespective of that, it is advisable to provide attributions wherever needed.

Generic

These websites contain everything: sprites, animations, models, sounds, music, and more.

Open Game Art

A crowd-sourced collection of all kinds of resources. Difficult to find what you're exactly looking for. Loading times are high

Kenney

Contains a collection of consistently high-quality assets grouped into convenient categories. Everything is free

Itch

Assets of the Itch store. Even the freebies are of good quality. Easily searchable and fast downloads

Game Dev Market

A commercially-driven community market. However, the freebies are nice too. Easily searchable

Art

Game Art 2D

Contains a lot of animated platformer sprites, GUI and tilesets. Most downloads require payment

CraftPix

A huge collection of broadly categorised game art. High quality freebies available. Easily searchable

Reiner's Tilesets

Contains a LOT of animated 2D isometric character sprites, 3D models and textures. Everything is free

Colors

Coolors

Generates color palettes and allows modification

Adobe Color Wheel

Generates color palettes based on a selected harmony rule

Canva

A searchable list of color palettes

Color hunt

List of color palettes, based on theme

Fonts

Google Fonts

The world's largest collection of free fonts

Free Fonts

A list of fonts based on theme

Font Library

A library of fonts, sorted by family

DaFont

Contains a bunch of fancy fonts

Icons

Game Icons

A huge inventory of crowd-sourced icons

Launcher Icons

Icons influenced from Android launcher

Flat Icons

A categorised list of icons and stickers

Noun Project

A collection of outlined and silhoutte icons

Images

Freepik

A huge collection of all sorts of images. Most are free, but require attribution. Easily searchable and conveniently categorised

Open ClipArt

A collection of 170 thousand free cliparts of all sorts. Searchable but not categorised.

Free Images

Stock photos and Royalty-free images

Morgue File

Images of nature and objects. Sorted by tags

Stick PNG

Free transparent PNGs

Vecteezy

Free vector art and stock photos

Models

Poly Pizza

A collection of free, low-poly models

Blender 3D Models

Crowd-sourced repository of Blender models

Free 3D

A bunch of free (and paid) 3D models

Music and Sound

Freesound

Crowd-sourced collection of all kinds of music and sound effects

Film Music

Freely available high-quality music productions of Kevin MacLeod

Musopen

Royalty-free instrumental and classical music

Melodicloud

A genre-grouped collection of paid music for games

Game Sounds

Game sounds in public domain

Pac DV

Categorised sound effects for free

Sound Bible

Free sound effects and sound clips

Free SFX

A free collection of categorised SFX

Textures

Poly Haven

A public library of HDRIs and textures

Texture King

Free stock textures, inspired by natural materials

Free PBR

Donwload free PBR materials and textures

Tools

Mixamo

Animator of 3D characters

Game Idea Generator

An abstract, randomised game idea generator

JSFXR

An SFX generator, pretuned for common sound effects

Fake Music Generator

Generates entire albums of fake music
