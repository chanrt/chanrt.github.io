Online Calculator

I desperately wanted an easily accessible calculator that can not only evaluate typed-in expressions, but also has inbuilt scientific constants and supports certain special functions that are not commonly seen in calculators. I finally gave up my search and developed it on my own.

Asian Physics Olympiad - 2022

India was the host of Asian Physics Olympiad - 2022. I was a part of the academic contigent of the organizer. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the experimental section of the olympiad could not take place offline. Hence, I played a pivotal role in developing a simulated lab environment, thereby enabling participants from 30+ countries to attempt the high stakes exam remotely.
